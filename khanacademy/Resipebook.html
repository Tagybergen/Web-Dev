Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5 cups
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2
Sugar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter.

Step 2: Heat a pan and cook pancakes on both sides until golden.

Recipe #2: Caesar Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Caesar dressing 3 tbsp
Grated Parmesan 2 tbsp

Step 1: Wash and chop lettuce, then place it in a bowl.

Step 2: Add croutons, dressing, and Parmesan. Toss well.
